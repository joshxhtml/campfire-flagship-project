Constants:
	Hole Locking starts on round 10 and every 5 round you lose another hole (hole locking effects an n number of random holes per round)
	
Starting Values:
	Shop starts at round 5 and appears every 5 rounds after that, can be decreased to 2 via a powerup\
	3 balls per round, can also be increased +1 per extra ball powerup
	
Gameplay Loop:
	First 5 rounds are vinilla, all it is is shooting the ball to get points. 
	Shop appears after round 5 and will continue every 5 round (see shop value), ability to get extra balls or mulitpliers
	Hole Locking starts at round 10, see hole locking constant, will allow to buy holes back evntually
